## Goals

1. Replace a basic, thin-content Zyro/Hostinger site with a premium, professional online presence
2. Generate consistent, high-quality leads from the right clients (homeowners, architects, developers)
3. Pre-qualify visitors — the site should signal premium pricing so budget clients self-select out
4. Enable targeted Google Ads campaigns per service page (future phase)
5. Rank in local Google search for heritage and architectural masonry in inner Melbourne suburbs

---

## Target Audience

**Primary:**
- High-end residential homeowners (inner Melbourne)
- Architects
- Builders and developers
- Commercial clients

**Secondary:**
- Government projects (via builder arrangements)

**Not targeting:**
- Property managers
- Budget-conscious clients
- Small patch repair jobs (letterboxes, 10–15 brick repairs)

## Tone of Voice

- **Premium, confident, and refined** — never salesy or pushy
- **Authoritative** — David is an expert; the copy speaks from that position
- **Understated** — quality speaks for itself; nothing loud or boastful
- **Direct** — clear sentences, no fluff, no trades clichés
- See `docs/content-notes.md` for full rules

---

## Design Direction

- **Feel:** Luxury architecture studio / heritage restoration firm — NOT a trades website
- **Palette:** Warm earthy browns — see `docs/content-notes.md` for hex codes
- **Layout:** Clean, minimal, generous negative space, image-led
- **Typography:** Elegant serif (Cormorant Garamond) for headings; clean sans (Jost) for body
- **Inspiration:** Verde Homes (primary), Abode Restoration, KJ Architecture, White Fox Real Estate
- **Logo:** White wordmark on dark — supplied PNG in `/public/logo/`
