## FAQ SECTION

**Q: Can you restore a heritage chimney on an older Melbourne home?**
A: Yes. Heritage chimney restoration is one of David's specialities. He has restored chimneys on period homes across Hawthorn, Camberwell, Canterbury, and surrounding suburbs, including a 1915 chimney that required full repointing, partial rebuilding, and gas flue removal. He works carefully to preserve the original character of the chimney while ensuring it is structurally sound.

**Q: Do you build new chimney stacks as well as restore existing ones?**
A: Yes. Goodwin Bricklaying builds new chimney stacks for residential and commercial clients, as well as restoring and repairing existing stacks. David has completed chimney projects ranging from domestic residential work through to large-scale commercial stacks, including a 40-metre stack in Collingwood.

**Q: Can you build a custom pizza oven?**
A: Yes. David builds handcrafted brick pizza ovens for residential clients. Each oven is constructed to function properly and to complement its surrounding space, whether that's a backyard entertaining area, a kitchen extension, or an architectural outdoor setting.

**Q: Do you handle fireplace repairs and modifications?**
A: Yes. David carries out heritage fireplace restoration, repair, repointing, and modification work for residential and architectural clients. If you have a period fireplace that needs attention, or want to modify an existing fireplace as part of a renovation, get in touch and David will assess what's involved.
