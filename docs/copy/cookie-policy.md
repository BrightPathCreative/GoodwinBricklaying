# Cookie Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

This Cookie Policy explains how Goodwin Bricklaying (ABN 87 434 328 744) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on goodwinbricklaying.com.au.

---

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you interact with it.

---

### Cookies We Use

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Provider |
|---|---|---|
| Essential | Required for the website to function correctly (e.g. form submissions) | Goodwin Bricklaying |
| Analytics | Understand how visitors use the site — pages visited, time on site, referral source | Google Analytics 4 |
| Marketing (if applicable) | Track ad performance if Google Ads campaigns are active | Google Ads |

---

### Google Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymised usage data. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics cookies. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time using the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

---

### Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website.

For instructions on managing cookies in your browser:
- [Google Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Mozilla Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences)
- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac)
- [Microsoft Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)
